# Privacy Policy for Magnet Remote

**Last Updated:** February 2025

## Overview

Magnet Remote is a macOS utility that forwards magnet links to your personal download server. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Magnet Remote:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not send data to any third-party services
- Does not store or transmit your magnet links to us

## Local Storage

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Server configuration** (host, port, protocol) - Stored in macOS User Defaults
- **Username** - Stored in macOS User Defaults
- **Password** - Stored securely in macOS Keychain
- **Recent magnets** - Stored locally for your convenience (last 10 links)
- **Preferences** - Notification settings, launch at login preference

## Network Activity

Magnet Remote only connects to:

- **Your configured download server** - To send magnet links and test connections

No other network connections are made. We do not operate any servers that receive data from this app.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

## Your Control

You can:

- Clear your recent magnets history at any time
- Remove your saved password from Keychain via macOS Keychain Access
- Delete app preferences by removing the app

## Children's Privacy

Magnet Remote does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at: https://github.com/pgpillar/MagnetRemote

---

**Summary:** Magnet Remote runs entirely on your device. Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your information.
